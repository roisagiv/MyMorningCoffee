Nicole Bass (television personality)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Nicole Bass is a British reality television personality from Essex, known primarily for appearing in the ITVBe reality series The Only Way Is Essex.[1] In 2017, she appeared in the sixth series of Ex on the Beach.[2]

Career[edit]

In October 2015, Nicole joined the cast of semi-reality television programme The Only Way is Essex for the sixteenth series.[3] but made her final appearance during the seventeenth series in April 2016.[citation needed]

In January 2017, she appeared in the sixth series of Ex on the Beach,[4] where she was brought in as the ex-girlfriend of Josh Ritchie.[citation needed]
